# Gameplay 

> Il faudra parcourir de multiples donjons à la recherche d'objets/reliques afin de soit faire avancer votre quête, soit pouvoir fabriquer et améliorer votre équipement.
> Dans les types de donjons on retrouvera des tombeaux anciens, des mausolées, des temples en ruine, des grottes, des forteresses, des mines maudites et des villages abandonnés ou non.

## Gameplay loop
- Explore dungeons (roguelite style) as a warrior or a werewolf
- Craft objects as a blacksmith
- Find preys and consume blood of different creatures to improve your health, strenght, stamina
- Try not to be suspicious in your hometown

___________
⚠️The character progresses either with the improvement of its stuff, or the improvement of its constition (or both), it's up to the player :
  - Explore dungeons to discover relics and objects taht may improve your stuff as a blacksmith
  - Rampage and slaughter villages as a werewolf to drink blood out of your ennemies and strengthen your body and health
Of course both ways work complementarily to become more powerful, should choose so 
__________

 ## Combat 
 ### Human (warrior) 
 - Switch between 2 different sets of weapons
 - Normal and heavy attack
 - Protect (with shield or weapon) 
 - Protect maintained + heavy attack 
 - Heal with potion
 - Roll
 - 3 "styles of combat" or "postures" :
    - Normal (Weapon in 1 hand + shield)
    - Quick (Same but faster with less range)
    - Heavy (Take the weapon in one hand, more range and damage) 

### Loup-Garou
- Switch between Bipeb or quadriped ( faster with four legs)
- Normal and heavy attack
- Protect maintained + heavy attack = big range attack by jumping on the foe
- Heal by eating ennemies
- Dash 
- 3 "styles of combat" :
   - Normal : circular claw attack and grab to finish and drink lood from the foe (4 legs : bite and attack on neck)
   - fast : circular claw attack (less range and damages but quicker) and vertical claw attack (jump forwar and bite)
   - heavy : claw attack (slower but better damages and range) and vertical punch (same)

## Craft 
- Create or improve objects (equipments, weapon)
- "Runes" to carve into the objects
- Materials : 
   - Iron, steel, copper, gold, silver, Dimeterium 
   - Wood, hardwood
   - fabric et leather
   - Ivory, bone 
   - coal
- Possible to alloy metals with other metals and/or ither materials
